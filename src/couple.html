the story


Hayley and Oliver did, in fact, meet after Hayley got a job at Whitcoulls Lambton Quay where Oliver was working. Actually, they first "met" when Hayley arrived for her interview and asked the handsome young man at the counter if he could please radio for the manager. Oliver later told Hayley that he had hoped she would get the job, as he thought she was pretty cute!

Over Hayley's first month of employment the pair would often talk on breaks (and when they were supposed to be talking to customers) about philosophy, getting back into music, and book reccommendations. They decided to hang out on a Friday, their day off, to play some music together. Oliver brought vegan garlic bread, and Hayley was instantly smitten.




The next day, Hayley decided to play it cool and not immediately text Oliver, when she received a text from him, asking her to go see a play or a movie. All the plays they found had sold out, so they went and saw Wonder Woman together, in classic first date fashion. Afterwards, they went to the Library (the bar, not the book distributary). And the rest is history!

Four months on, the two decided to move in together into a tiny studio apartment, close to where their Master of Software Development course would be. They studied together for a year, and after much hard work and MANY burgers, the two finished the course and moved into different jobs in the tech industry.




In July 2019, the two took a trip to Maruia Hot Springs in the South Island. It was cold and rainy as heck, but it was to be the most wonderful trip of their lives so far. Oliver proposed at a lookout over the valleys of Lewis Pass as the mist rolled over the mountains. And Hayley said yes!


The two have since continued to build a life together with all the trappings of millennial domesticity - a robot vacuum called Rover who keeps the place clean, a bamboo plant that Hayley lovingly nurtures, and a Google Home that turns the lights off and on for them.


That's just the first few chapters of the story, folks. Join them in Christchurch for the next!
